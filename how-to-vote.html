How to Vote

Vote for your favorite pitch!

  1. Search for awesomevote in your app store (no spaces)
  2. Download the app
  3. Open the app
  4. Click on a team name
  5. Rate them!
  6. Voting is only open during that team’s presentation
  7. If you can’t find or download the app: go to awesomevote.xyz in your browser and vote.
